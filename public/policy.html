Leave policy

Earned leaves (EL)

It is recomonded that all earned leaves are applied for at least 2 weeks in advance and approved by your manager. And also logged in AKQA's time management system Workbook.
Provide information of this via email to your project manager and HR (gur.leaverequest@akqa.com).

  • Encashable only at the time of separation
  • Cannot be caried forward to next year
  • Half days not possible

Sick leaves

These are taken when you are unwell. For when someone else is unwell you can use EL or CL.
Sick leaves taken for more than 2 days consecutively or exceding 4 days in a month will require a certificate from a medical practitioner.
Drop an email to gur.leaverequest@akqa.com along with cc to your HR and line manager as soon as possible but no later than 9AM.

  • Not encashable
  • Cannot be caried forward to next year
  • Half days not possible

Casual leaves (CL)

For ad-hoc individual needs like attending family members in hospital, visiting passport office or attending condolence meeting.
CL cannot be clubbed with and other category of leave
No more than 2 CL's can be applied at a strech.

  • Not encashable
  • Cannot be caried forward to next year
  • Can be taken as half-days
